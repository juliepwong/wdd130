Hi everyone. My name is Julie Wong and the nickname that most people call me is Jewels. Some of my favorite things to do is go to Disneyland, watch movies with friends and family, and work on fun art projects.

What I like about BYU-I, is the online program. With the online program, I am able to make my school schedule work with my work schedule. I am pursuing a Bachelors in Science in Professional Studies. Which consists of Social Media Marketing, Graphic Design, and Web Frontend Design.

Some of the benefits I see about learning about careers in web design is, I will learn many new things and be able to help my friends better their websites that they have. I think it will also help me to possibly a better job that I can excel at.

I have been a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints my whole life. My favorite quote from the church leaders, would have to be how getting an education is an important blessing to have in our lives. Because of their suggestions, I finally went back to school to finish my education and get a Bachelors Degree. My favorite scripture would have to be D&C 68:6.
