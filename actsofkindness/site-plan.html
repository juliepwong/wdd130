Overview

Purpose

The purpose of Acts of Kindness is for those that are willing to do something nice for those that may be in need. Need of some love and kindness. With Acts of Kindness, everyone can be like a Good Samaritan and or one that wants to do a good deed.

Audience

My audience is for those of all ages. Everyone can be apart of helping those around them. Showing kindness and serving others with service projects.

Content

Home page

[Are you wanting to do more to help those in your community, but not sure how to? Well, look no further than Acts of Kindness. We are here to help you from serving in your local community to serving your fellow friends and family. We have tons of ideas that may help inspire you to help those around you.]

Images for the Home page

Serving

[Wanting to serve others, but not sure what to do? Well, we have tons of ideas of ways you can serve others. From volunteering at different places, all the way to coordinating projects to complete and give to others in need. Some of the places that you can volunteer at: food banks, senior homes, homeless shelters, local libraries, and so much more. With these places, there is always a need that can be met. With our page, you will have access to these different ideas and where you can go to serve. What is better than serving others than with friends? Well, we have tons of different ideas that you can do with your friends and families. Some ideas that can be done with friends and families are: hygiene kits, quilt tied blankets, canned food drive, toy drive, adopt a family for the holidays, and many more. All of these fun ideas can really help those in need. With going to places to volunteer and creating a volunteer project. It can give a person a great feeling to be serving others. The cool thing is, you don’t have to wait until the holidays to serve others in need. Serving others can be all year round. We can serve by ourselves or with others. No matter the group size, there is always different ways that a person can bring love to others. Love that those in need may need. Especially in times like today. A time where things can be hard with things changing in the world. From food prices to gas prices.]

Images for the Page 2

Be Kind

[Know of someone that may be having a hard time, but not sure how you can help. We have some great ideas that can help you brighten their day. Some of the best ways that you can help is sending them a thoughtful message. Bringing them their favorite treats. Taking them out to do something fun. Picknick in the park. Taking a walk. Even just talking with them can help. Those are just some ways that you can show kindness to those you know. There are so many other ways that you can be there for them and we are here to help. See someone that you don’t know that might need your help? There are lots of different ways that you can help them too. It can be a simple smile and a wave. Paying it forward by purchasing their food or drinks. Buying them groceries if they need it. A simple note of letting them know that someone cares about them. Even just asking them if they are ok and if they need anything. We never know what someone may be going through. So even if it is a small random act of kindness from giving a warm smile and telling them to have an awesome day, it can be a world to that person. To be there for someone can be the best feeling to have. The feeling of knowing that we can be there for someone is the best feeling we could ever have. If you need help knowing some ideas of how you can show kindness to others. Come check out our page for ideas and some free print outs that you can use.]
